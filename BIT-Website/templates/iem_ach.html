Besides the above, engineers are necessarily exposed to the industrial environment during the 8th semester, for duration of 8 to 10 weeks. During this period they are made to undertake and enter into practical arenas of day to day industrial problems. Projects done by IEM students are:-

1. Human resource management in ITI
2. Work place design
3. Review of MIS in BEML
4. Production scheduling in engineering industry - a case study
5. Simulation of plant layout using QUEST
6. Formation of cells in plunger area of MICO plant
