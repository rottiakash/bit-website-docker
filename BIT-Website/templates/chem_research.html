Research Initiatives: The department has a well-established Research Centre recognized by Visveswaraya Technological University since 1998. The centre has received 7 research grants from various external funding agencies like Department of Science and Technology (DST), New Delhi, Department of Atomic Energy (DAE), Mumbai, Heavy Water Board, Mumbai and VTU. The centre is engaged in academic research in various fields of Applied Chemistry like heterogeneous catalysis, adsorption, reaction kinetics, material science, alternate energy sources, synthetic chemistry and medicinal chemistry. More than 50 papers are published in peer reviewed, reputed international journals of high impact factor and one patent is filed. 10 scholars have acquired Ph. D. degree from the centre. The research centre has state-of-the-art facilities for preparation of new chemical materials, characterization and study of catalytic activity. These facilities are being extended for engineering students for their project works.
