DEPARTMENT OF MATHEMATICS

About

The Mathematics department was established in the year 1979 with three teaching faculty. Currently, the department has 12 teaching and 2 non – teaching staff. Our faculty members specialize in various areas – Fluid Mechanics, Graph theory, Mathematical Modeling, Transportation GIS and applied mathematics.

The department is headed by Dr. K.M. Roopa. There are four faculty members with Doctoral degree and four faculty members are pursuing doctoral program. As a whole, the department has an active involvement in organizing the workshops and conferences on Mathematical sciences & related softwares.



Dr K M Roopa

Prof. & Head of the Dept

Vision and Mission

VISION

MISSION

  • Applying mathematics as a tool to model and solve Engineering problems.
  • Creating a pool of talent to undertake research and development.
