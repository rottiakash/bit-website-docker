Research

Research and development (R&D) is of great importance in technical field as the level of competition and innovation. Research and development consists of investigative activities that a technical person chooses to conduct with the intention of making a discovery that can either lead to the development of new products or procedures, or to improvement of existing products or procedures. R&D is one of the means by which technology can experience future growth by developing new products or processes to improve.

The department of Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering/Instrumentation Technology is for the innovation/improvements. The department offers research in the major field of Digital Image Processing, Biomedical Signal processing, Adhoc Networking and Neural Networking & Wavelet Transform.
