Infrastructure:

The department possesses up to date equipment and software packages to deep the students abreast with the latest trends and requirements of the ever changing industrial environment

Laboratories:

The department has well equipped Laboratories affording students practical experiences in handling machines and theoretical know how.
Electrical Machines Lab: Include AC/DC generator and motors, inductions and synchronous motors, transformers.

Microcontroller Lab : a full fledged microcontroller lab , that offers interaction with 8051 model.

Electrical Measurement lab: Equipped with latest technologies in electrical energy measurement and instrumentation such as kelvin bridges, flux meter, universal measuring bridges, energy meter and digital RMS meters

Relay & High voltage lab: Experiments on measurement of high voltage Study of various current & voltage Relay Characteristics using testing kits,high voltage systems etc.,is carried out.

Power electronics lab: Experiments involving study of SCRs, chopper and inverter circuits etc are taken up. study of electric drives and speed control of motor arecarried out.

Controls Lab: Practical exercises on analysis of DC servomotors, feedback mechanisms, stepper motors characteristics are conducted.

Digital signal processing Lab: Using MAT-LAB, processing of various types of digital signal is done.

Power system simulation / pspice lab: The lab houses MI-POWER software for load flow studies, fault analysis , steady state and transient state stability studies, optimality of performance study, system interconnection and their computer simulation etc.

AEC/DEC Labs: study of characteristics of transistors, diodes, FET,MOSFET, and opamps are taken up. Realization of flip-flops, counters, multiplexers, demultiplexers, encoder,, decoder, ADC/DAC, multivibrators etc. are done.
