Infrastructure/Facilities

The central library is housed in two separate wings of main building with a carpet area of over 1204sq mts and is located in the center of the campus. The library services and resources are mentioned below.

Ground Floor (North wing)

 

Digital Section

Online access to the e-resources subscription through VTU e-resources consortium of IEEE/IEL, Springer Link, ASCE, ASME, Federated Search Knimbus.be accessed VTU Consortium e- resources (Online Journals) provided on Remote access.

Circulation Section:

    • Stack area for books in all disciplines are shelved as per the classification number with open access
    • OPAC Services
    • Document Circulation Counter

 

 

Book Bank Section:

 

  • The facility is provided for category/disability students, students are entitled to borrow three additional books.

Ground/ First Floor (East wing)

  • Librarian Chamber
  • Periodical Section: Back volumes of Journals have been bound and arranged in subject wise for reference and Technical magazines are displayed
  • Reading Room: Newspapers and general magazines displayed in reading room
  • Reference Section : The Reference section of the library has collection of international editions of reference books, handbooks, encyclopedias, competitive books and various reference documents

 

Electronic Surveillances

  • 18 CCTV cameras are installed in the library premises as security measure

 

Referral services:

  • Students and researchers in need of diverted information have been referred to use other reputed library like IISc, NAL, CMTI, and IIMB through institutional membership

 

User awareness Programme:-

  • Library facility and orientation programme services will be conducted for the new students

 

Reprographic services/ Centralized:

  • Centralized Reprographic services available in the campus
  • Scanning/ Print , Internet, Intranet and downloading facilities are available
