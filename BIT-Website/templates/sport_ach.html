SUB: BREIF REPORT Of ALL INDIA INTER UNIVERSITY, AQUATIC WOMEN CHAMPIONSHIP FOR THE YEAR 2018-19

The above championship was conducted at Basavanagudi Aquatic Centre and Nisha Millet Swimming Academy Ulsoor Bengaluru. from 2nd to 4 th November 2018 by Visveswaraya Technological University Belagavi in association with BIT Bangalore and Technical support was Officiating is done by Karnataka Swimming Association. Previous day there was a Managers meeting regarding brief details about the championship at Bangalore Institute of Technology campus, Bengaluru.

Total 300 participants from 85 university across India has participated in this championship day one started with colorful march fast and grand Opening ceremony in presence of Chief Guest Dr G Parameshwara Deputy Chief minister, Govt of Karnataka and Shri Basavaraja Horatti, Honb’le Chairman ,Karnataka Legislative Council, Govt of Karnataka, Dr. H.N Jagannath Reddy Registrar of VTU Belagavi and Chairman, President and Vice President of KSA andDr. P Chinnappa Reddy, guest, Dr. Gulab Singh Ruhal Observar of All India Inter University Aquatic (Women) Championship 2018-19 Shri H S Ashokananda IAS (Retd) ,Administrator of Rajya Vokkaligara sangha Dr. M.U Aswath Principal BIT, Dr. Arif Ali Khan Organizing Secretary VTU Belagavi Prof. Shankara Gowda, Sports Secretary BIT and Organizing Secretary Sri K. T .Dasegowda, PED BIT and Convener and all the Officials of VTU and All the Organizing Committee Chairman’s & Members of BIT.

Food and accommodation arranged for all participants, Coach, Managers and officials. All the three days events held successfully without any problem and every one where very happy with the facility provided by VTU & BIT
