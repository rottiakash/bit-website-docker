DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE & ENGINEERING

About

In KARNATAKA STATE, BIT was the pioneer in establishing the COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING department, with affiliation to BANGALORE UNIVERSITY in the year 1981. The syllabus was framed by then H.O.D. Prof. M. Ramakrishna, ever since the department has adopted and evolved with the fast pace of technology.

The Department offers Under-Graduation [UG], Post-Graduation [PG] and Research under VTU. PG programme, M. Tech in Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 2008.

The Department has excellent infrastructure facilities with six labs, such as Algorithm & Data Structures, Database & Networking Labs, Web Technologies, System Software & Operating System Lab and The Microprocessor Lab, to prepare the techno savvy’s to conquer the demands of the IT industry, R&D organizations and computer related areas. The objective is to educate, train and develop world class research and IT professionals with a mastery of not only hardware and software skills but also soft skills for professional success that helps 95% of the Computer Science students to get placed in top software companies like JP Morgan, Akamai, Accenture, Robert Bosch, IGate etc. Most of the students are offered higher pay packages from software giants like Linkedin, Adobe, Netapp, Cisco, Oracle etc.

The Department offers an excellent academic environment with a team of highly qualified and dedicated faculties with most of the staff’s having doctoral degrees. Many other staff members are currently involved in doing their search work and are involved in taking care of several software projects. The department of Computer Science and Engineering constantly organizes seminars and other interactive programs to keep the student community updated about the latest happening in the IT-sector.

The Department is currently headed by Dr. Asha .T B.E., M.E., Ph.D

Vision and Mission

Vision

To be a center of excellence in computer engineering education, empowering graduates as highly skilled professionals.

Mission

• To provide a platform for effective learning with emphasis on technical excellence.
• To train the students to meet current industrial standards and adapt to emerging technologies.
• To instill the drive for higher learning and research initiatives.
• To inculcate the qualities of leadership and Entrepreneurship.

Program Educational Objectives (PEO’s)

1. Provide graduates with both fundamental and advanced concepts of computer science and engineering for acquiring knowledge.
2. To prepare the graduates to apply computer engineering knowledge to build professional career.
3. To enable students to engage in lifelong learning with the ability to involve in higher studies and research.
4. To encourage graduates to imbibe professional ethics and soft skills.

Program Specific Outcomes

1: The graduates of the program will have the ability to build software products by applying theoretical concepts and programming skills.
2: The graduates of the program will have the ability to pursue higher studies and Research in the modern computing era.
