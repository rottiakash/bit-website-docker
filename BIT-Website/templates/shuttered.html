Shuttered BIT

The club has been started around the year 2011 by the photography enthusiasts of our college. The club has actively participated in covering all the events including the ROTAFEST 2K17 and the events of LEO CLUB too. The club has covered all the events organized during the College Fest-MANTHAN in the year 2018-19. The Club aims to bring out the Creative Photographers in Everyone and to build a Community Of Like Minded Photographers. Shuttered club conducts Photo walks helping them to develop their skills to create a community of likeminded people.
