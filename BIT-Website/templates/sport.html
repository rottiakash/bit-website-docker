About

The Department of Sports & Games in BIT was established in the year 1981 with affiliation to BANGALORE UNIVERSITY, under the leadership of Mr. Lingaiah, Sports officer BIT. Mr. K. T. Dasegowda is the Physical Education Director since 1980. Department of Physical Education & Sports Science of BIT serves an integral part of student and staff life on campus. The aim is to provide a broad spectrum of sports, recreation and leisure activities for students, and staff Members. The department is striving to provide services and programs that stimulate growth, development and retention of students in a contemporary and safe environment that develops fitness and wellness, social interaction and leadership opportunities.

Vision and Mission

Vision

To promote “Sports for ALL” and be the state of art facility center to the students and staff for sound physical and psychological health.

Mission

· To create state of art Infrastructure for Sports and Games.

· To motivate the Students and Staff for active participation in sports and games for the overall personality development.

· To build a comprehensive program that enhances student learning, team building, leadership qualities and wellness.

Objectives of Sports and Games is to Promote

. Health : reducing stress and strain on body and mind.

. General Wellbeing : Physical Education enhances feelings of happiness.

. Good Habits : Physical Education early on ensures that adults continue exercising for many years to come.

. Team players : Physical Education makes participants into good team players.

. Self-help : a great way to help ourselves with our issues.

. Overall Development : all round development, not just academic

We Strive to Meet Our Mission by

• Easy to Follow, Precise Steps – By following these steps, you have clear, focused goals which make it simple to train and create the drive towards sports.
• Motivational Training, Motivational talks.
• Goal-Oriented Instructions, Increase confidence in competition and a winning performance.
