Product Development

  • Mr Hansum Krishna, final year student of the Department of ISE developed AgroSquad which is a unit of wirelessly communicating autonomous sub-systems that make use of image processing, and embedded systems with modular sensors to survey and manage agricultural landscapes for optimizing crop production. Multirotor-copter based areal imagery detects soil variations, crop quality and pest/fungal infestation - which aren’t apparent at eye level, while the ground level probe capsule gathers soil-specific parametrical measurements for ensuring crop quality and yield.
  • Mr Hansum Krishna also developed Project DriX which is an advanced voice-enabled driver-assistance AI for human-driven vehicles, which uses driving behavior recognition system, comprising a set of sensors embedded in the driver’s console – discretizing driving patterns on a highly individualized level.
  • Mr Shriyan, 2nd year student of the department of ISE build an unmanned ground vehicle (UGV) capable of being controlled using hand gestures which benefits the disabled persons. As those persons with the hand gesture moves to the direction they want to go without pressing any buttons.
