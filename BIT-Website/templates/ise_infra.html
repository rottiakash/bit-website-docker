Infrastructure and Facilities available:

Department has enough class and tutorial rooms, there are 3 UG class rooms,2 PG class room with most of them equipped with projectors, one PG lab and research lab and there are five well equipped laboratories. The library is equipped with books, journals and student project reports, for quick reference for both students and staff.

The Departmental Laboratories - Algorithm & Data Structures, Database & Networking Labs, Web Technologies, system software & operating system lab and the Microprocessor Lab are equipped with state of the art technologies. The systems work on a dual processor capable Intel-Xeon server, enabling students to access resources across the department.

The internet facility is provided by the Department with the speed of 10Mbps(1:1) leased line from Aircel for all systems available in the lab.
