Aikya Club

AIKYA is a youth initiative of The Akshaya Patra Foundation. AIKYA provides a platform for the students to build and hone their leadership and communication capabilities, thereby creating social leaders, who bring out a positive development in our country. We work around government schools by organizing cleanliness campaigns, tree  plantation drives and, school education programs, bringing in an overall impact in the ecosystem of the government schools.
